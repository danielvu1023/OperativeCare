Core Capabilities

Built for the speed
of clinical decisions.

📡

Real-Time Vitals

Six vital parameters updated every second. Heart rate, blood pressure (systolic + diastolic), SpO₂, temperature, and respiratory rate — all tracked with sparkline trend visualization.

1s update interval
🧠

Intelligent Triage

Automatic severity classification across four levels — Normal, Warning, Urgent, Critical — with immediate routing to the appropriate care provider and clinical reasoning attached to every alert.

4-tier severity engine
👥

Role-Based Access

Nurses, charge nurses, residents, and attendings each see only the alerts that route to them. No alert fatigue from irrelevant noise. Provider-specific inboxes with acknowledge and dismiss workflows.

4 provider roles

Severity System

Four levels.
Zero ambiguity.

Every alert carries a severity level that tells the right clinician exactly what action is needed — before they read a single word.

● Normal
All vitals within expected post-operative parameters. Routine observation continues.
→ Nurse (Floor)
▲ Warning
One or more vitals approaching clinical thresholds. Heightened monitoring indicated.
→ Charge Nurse
!! Urgent
Vitals beyond warning thresholds. Physician assessment and intervention required.
→ Resident Physician
✕ Critical
Life-threatening parameter deviation. Rapid Response Team activation required immediately.
→ Attending + Rapid Response
